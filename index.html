    Our Subsidiaries

    Tara Foods

    Tara Foods Ltd is dedicated to delivering fresh, high-quality food products to households and businesses across the region. with focus on sustainability and local sourcing, we ensure that every product is crafted with care and delivered with passion

    Rayen Fresh

    At Rayen Fresh, we are dedicated to bringing the freshest and healthiest produce from farm to table. Our commitment to sustainability ensures that every product is ethically sourced, and we work closely with farmers to ensure fair trade practices. Our range includes fruits, vegetables, and organic produce that meet the highest quality standards.

    Rayen Dairy

    At Rayen Dairy, we are committed to providing premium dairy products sourced from the best farms in the region. Our focus is on delivering fresh, nutritious, and sustainably produced milk, yogurt, and cheese to meet your dietary needs.

    Rayen Trading

    Rayen Poultry is dedicated to raising and delivering the finest poultry products in the region. Our farms follow best practices in poultry farming, ensuring that our chickens are raised in a humane, healthy environment. From free-range eggs to high-quality meat, Rayen Poultry provides nutritious options for your family.
