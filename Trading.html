Welcome To Rayen Trading

About Rayen Trading

Rayen Poultry is dedicated to raising and delivering the finest poultry products in the region. Our farms follow best practices in poultry farming, ensuring that our chickens are raised in a humane, healthy environment. From free-range eggs to high-quality meat, Rayen Poultry provides nutritious options for your family.

Explore Our Services

Our Import & Export Services

Import Services

We source and import the highest quality goods from trusted suppliers around the world. Our team ensures compliance with international standards and efficient delivery.

Export Services

Our export services enable local businesses to reach international markets. We handle all aspects of logistics and ensure your products meet destination market requirements.

Logistics Management

From storage to shipping, we manage the entire logistics process to ensure timely delivery and seamless transactions for both importers and exporters.

Key Product Categories

At Rayen Poultry, we believe in ethical farming. Our chickens are raised in a free-range environment with access to open spaces and fresh feed. We ensure their well-being through humane practices, contributing to healthier and higher-quality poultry products.

Agricultural Products

From fresh produce to bulk commodities, Rayen Trading specializes in agricultural exports and imports.

Manufactured Goods

We trade a wide variety of manufactured goods, including machinery, textiles, and consumer products.

Consumer Goods

We offer a broad range of consumer goods, including electronics, apparel, and home essentials, for both import and export markets.
