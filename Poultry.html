About Rayen Poultry

Rayen Poultry is dedicated to raising and delivering the finest poultry products in the region. Our farms follow best practices in poultry farming, ensuring that our chickens are raised in a humane, healthy environment. From free-range eggs to high-quality meat, Rayen Poultry provides nutritious options for your family.

Our Commitment to Animal Welfare

At Rayen Poultry, we believe in ethical farming. Our chickens are raised in a free-range environment with access to open spaces and fresh feed. We ensure their well-being through humane practices, contributing to healthier and higher-quality poultry products.

Humane Treatment

Our poultry is raised with care, ensuring the best quality of life for each bird.

Natural Feed

We feed our chickens with natural, nutrient-rich diets, free from antibiotics and hormones

Free-Range Environment

Our birds have access to fresh air, sunlight, and open spaces, promoting their health and well-being
