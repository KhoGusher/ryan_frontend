Slogan

Trusted partners in growth.

Rayen Ltd, founded in 2020, offers services such as management consulting, venture capital, debt, and private equity financing. We focus on helping businesses grow suistainably and ethically, with a mission to positively impact communities.

Guided by trust, integrity, and teamwork, our leadership team brings vast experience in strategy, finance, and business development.

Rayen Ltd is based in Lilongwe, Malawi, and serves both local and international clients.
